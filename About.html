About

Hello. I'm Matt, an ex-software-engineer and computing omnivore who now pursues offensive information security as a career and hobby. This blog is meant to contain informational articles about hacking and computer exploitation techniques. It's largely my scratchpad and a way to share what I learn with the world. I'm often told that I'm a straightforward person of few words--until you get me talking about something I love. Then the few words can bloom into a few thousand. I hope at least some of them assist you in solving a problem or stir your curiosity. Thank you for reading.
